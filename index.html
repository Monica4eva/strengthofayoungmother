Our Mission

Every Mother Deserves Support

We understand what it's like to feel alone in motherhood. The sleepless nights filled with worry. The fear about providing for your child. The weight of society's judgment. The dreams that seem impossible.

That's why Strength of a Young Mother exists—to ensure no mother walks this journey alone. We're here to transform your challenges into opportunities, your pain into purpose, and your dreams into reality.

Founded in 2014, we're now setting an ambitious goal: to impact 150+ young mothers in 2026, building a community where mothers find their voice, reclaim their power, and create lasting legacies for their children.

Because when we empower one mother, we empower an entire generation.

The Challenge

With unemployment, abuse and crime on the rise, single mothers are overwhelmed, overburdened, unemployed, and lack support.

Motherhood is like a transformation that can go either way—we want it to take us HIGHER.

Our Solution

We provide holistic support—from emotional wellness to business training, from legal assistance to spiritual guidance. We walk with mothers every step of their journey.

Because when one mother rises, she lifts her entire community with her.

Mission

To actively work on encouraging and enabling young mothers to take active roles in their own personal transformation and the lives of their children.

Vision

To see young mothers reach their goals, heal from traumatic experiences, and lead by example while creating sustainable wealth.

Values

Accountability, Divine Femininity, Support, Self-Love, Self-Worth, Devotion, Passion, Independence, Hope, and Peace.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining STAYM

STAYM is open to all young mothers in Botswana who are looking for support, community, and empowerment. Whether you're a single mother, a student mother, or simply a young mother facing challenges, you belong here. We welcome mothers of all backgrounds, ages, and circumstances.

As a member, you'll receive holistic support including: psychosocial counseling, business training and mentorship, legal assistance, health and wellness programs, networking opportunities, access to micro-loans (for 12+ month members), and most importantly, a supportive community of women who understand your journey.

No! You don't need to have a business idea or be an entrepreneur to join. STAYM supports mothers in all aspects of life - whether you're looking for emotional support, education, health resources, or yes, business opportunities. We'll help you discover your path and walk it with you.

Micro-loans are available to members who have been active for 12+ months. These are small business loans with very low interest rates designed to help you start or grow your business. The application process is supportive and designed to set you up for success, not just give you money.

We are based in Gaborone, Botswana, but our community extends throughout the country. We hold regular meetups in Gaborone and host online events and forums so members from all regions can participate. Distance should never be a barrier to support.

Absolutely. We understand the sensitive nature of many situations our members face. All personal information, counseling sessions, and support group discussions are kept strictly confidential. Your privacy and safety are our top priorities.

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. However, we hope you'll give us a chance to address any concerns first. Our door is always open if you need to take a break and return later - once part of the STAYM family, always part of the family.
